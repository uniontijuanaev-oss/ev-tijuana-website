Baja California Charging Directory

A community-built directory to find charging stations across the whole state — Tijuana, Rosarito, Tecate, Ensenada, and Mexicali.

Playas de Tijuana Station

Tijuana
  • Location Jardines de Playas de Tijuana, C.P. 22500
  • Equipment Autel MaxiCharger Level 2 Commercial (up to 19.2 kW / 80 A)
  • Connector J1772 (adapter required for Tesla)
  • Pricing Dynamic, based on CFE peak/off-peak hours — check the app
  • Hours 24/7 Access

Xcelentric New City

Tijuana
⚡ Electric vehicle charging station · Zona Urbana Río · Open
  • Location Sótano 1 (Basement 1), directly underneath the glowing tower at the plaza — access through the Quartz Hotel structure
  • Hours Open 24/7 — standard plaza parking fee applies in addition to the electricity used

💡 Tip: You must download the "Autel Charge" app to initialize the session. Your account region must be set to Mexico — choosing a U.S. region or using a U.S. phone number may cause the app configuration to glitch.

Hotel Pueblo Amigo Plaza Casino Tijuana

Tijuana
⭐ 4.4 · 🏨 Hotel · Open
  • Area Zona Río, close to the San Ysidro border crossing
  • Location Main parking lot of the hotel plaza
  • Hours Accessible 24 hours a day

💡 Widely used by travelers coming across the border looking for a reliable spot to top off.

Plaza Alameda Otay Town Center

Tijuana
⭐ 4.6 · 🛍️ Shopping mall · Watts by Vemo network
  • Location Underground parking structure of the shopping complex
  • Hours Plaza hours: 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM

💡 Tip: Cellular signals can be weak in the underground parking area — download the charging provider's app before entering the basement.

Hyatt Place Tijuana

Tijuana
⭐ 4.5 · 🏨 Hotel
  • Location Hotel's dedicated parking area, Boulevard Agua Caliente
  • Hours Available 24/7

💡 An excellent stop for visitors staying overnight or stopping by the hotel's amenities.

How to Charge at Playas de Tijuana

  1. Park in the designated EV charging bay.
  2. Plug the J1772 connector into your vehicle's charge port.
  3. Open the EV Tijuana app or scan the QR code to authorize.
  4. Monitor battery diagnostics and safety in real-time.
