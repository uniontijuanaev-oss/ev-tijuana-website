Playas de Tijuana Station

Tijuana
🔌 Level 2
  • Location Jardines de Playas de Tijuana, C.P. 22500
  • Equipment Autel MaxiCharger Level 2 Commercial (up to 19.2 kW / 80 A)
  • Connector J1772 (adapter required for Tesla)
  • Pricing Dynamic, based on CFE peak/off-peak hours — check the app
  • Hours 24/7 Access

Xcelentric New City

Tijuana
🔌 Level 2
⚡ Electric vehicle charging station · Zona Urbana Río · Open
  • Location Sótano 1 (Basement 1), directly underneath the glowing tower at the plaza — access through the Quartz Hotel structure
  • Hours Open 24/7 — standard plaza parking fee applies in addition to the electricity used

💡 Tip: You must download the "Autel Charge" app to initialize the session. Your account region must be set to Mexico — choosing a U.S. region or using a U.S. phone number may cause the app configuration to glitch.

Regional Context

  • Coverage Baja California has approx. 200 charging points installed by the state government and the private sector (El Imparcial, 2024).
  • Expansion VEMO has installed new chargers in Otay and other areas of Baja California and Baja California Sur.
  • Connectors Many stations in community directories (Electromaps, PlugShare) are Tesla Destination chargers — non-Tesla EVs may need an adapter.

💡 This data comes from community directories (Electromaps, PlugShare) and may be incomplete or outdated — help us verify it!

How to Charge at Playas de Tijuana

  1. Park in the designated EV charging bay.
  2. Plug the J1772 connector into your vehicle's charge port.
  3. Open the EV Tijuana app or scan the QR code to authorize.
  4. Monitor battery diagnostics and safety in real-time.
